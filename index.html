1. Subway Surfers (2012 | 1B+ Downloads)

Subway Surfers is one of Android's top infinite runner games with super vibrant graphics. In this game, you play a character running away from an inspector and his dog after he catches you tagging the subway with graffiti. As you run on the tracks, you must dodge obstacles and collect coins, among other items, to help you escape.This game is known to introduce new cities with each update, which translates into new challenges and season hunts. Subway Surfers rewards you with in-game currency, characters, and power-ups like jetpacks, hoverboards, and magnets. These power-ups help you run a longer distance, hit higher scores, and give you an upper hand over your competition.

2. Candy Crush Saga (2012 | 1B+ Downloads)

The ubiquitous Candy Crush Saga will top your list if you are looking for a match-three puzzle game. The goal is simple; you must match three or more candies to score points and advance levels.Besides fancy animations, addictive gameplay, and endless levels, the game allows you to connect with other players via social platforms like Facebook. This way, you can share your progress with family and friends, on top of contesting for leaderboards.Additionally, Candy Crush Saga features special candy moves with which you can create special effects and power-ups to help you beat levels faster.If you love challenges, the game also sets specific scores to hit or limits the number of moves required to reach the next level. All in all, Candy Crush Saga remains addicting as much as it is entertaining.

3. Free Fire (2017 | 1B+ Downloads)

Free Fire is a battle royale game in which you join 49 other players and battle it for the last-man-standing position. During the game, you have to find weapons and supplies to help you face off your enemies.Each game session lasts 10 minutes, and as the time dwindles, the play area keeps shrinking, similar to PUBG. In addition, you get access to exclusive rewards in the game's battle pass system when you complete challenges. As a bonus, Free Fire has colorful graphics and an enjoyable gaming style.

4. My Talking Tom (2013 | 1B+ Downloads)

My Talking Tom presents you with a virtual pet cat, Tom, that you have to take care of just like a natural pet. The game is fun and interactive, and requires you to feed, groom, play with, and dress up Tom in different outfits. Even better, Tom can communicate with you through advanced AI technology for that interactive touch to the game. The game offers you various mini-games and activities you can play with Tom to pass the time. Some of these mini-games can be challenging, but they come in handy to help you keep Tom happy and healthy. Looking at the download stats, My Talking Tom has proven to be a great, fun, and engaging game, no matter your age.

5. Temple Run 2 (2013 | 500M+ Downloads)

Temple Run 2 is an Indiana Jones-inspired endless runner in which you have just stolen a golden idol and have to outrun the demonic monkeys that want it back. You traverse exciting environments as you escape, crossing rivers, cliffs, mines, forests, grassy paths, aqueducts, zip lines, narrow bridges, and more.Controlling your character is simple. Run, jump, slide, and turn as needed, to beat the challenging levels while collecting coins. You will also get power-ups and special powers during the game to aid your escape. Overall, Temple Run 2 radiates addictive gameplay fused with eye-catching graphics.
